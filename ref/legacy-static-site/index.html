Simplicity and efficiency guide everything I do. They create the freedom to focus on what really matters: solving problems well, empowering others, and enjoying the process.

Work

What I Do

    Founder at Already There Travel: I created a travel management system that supports Managed Services Providers handle large-scale, global projects that span continents. Leading a team that operates across languages, cultures, and time zones, we solve logistical problems before they arise—saving our clients time, money, and stress while becoming an essential part of their operations.
    Social Impact Investor: Through PrestaMio and Nativ Capital, I provide funding and guidance to entrepreneurs in underserved communities in Costa Rica. By combining capital with strategic advice, I help businesses grow, creating jobs and opportunities that strengthen the local economy.
    Community Servant and Leader: I founded Visit Cahuita and The Cahuita Community Group Alliance to connect people, support local businesses, and strengthen Costa Rica’s Southern Caribbean region. These efforts have raised funds for urgent medical care, supported food kitchens, and provided direct aid to families in need. By bringing local and international communities together, we’re driving collaboration and creating lasting change.
